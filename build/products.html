Portable chargers & panels

Thanks to a super-sized 40W solar panel charger, the Solaqua-mini offers a beefy 2.8A (Amps) of power capable of re-charging smartphones, tablets and powering any outdoor devices such as electronic road signs, small garden pumps and radios. Built-in to the Solaqua-mini is a 5V/2A charging port for a 5V gadget and a 18V DC output (18V/2A) for charging a laptop or other 18V powered devices. Fortunately, all that extra juice doesn’t mean an unwieldy size.It maintains a slim and foldable profile that’s easy to carry around. It can go in or on a backpack with the included carabiner that hooks directly to a bag.
